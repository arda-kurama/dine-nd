Privacy Policy for DineND

Effective Date: June 25, 2025

1. Overview

DineND is a mobile application that helps university students view dining hall menus and plan meals based on nutritional information. We are committed to respecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

At this time, DineND does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

  • No login or account creation is required.
  • No personal identifiers, location data, or device information is collected.
  • No analytics or tracking tools are currently in use.

3. AI Services

DineND uses OpenAI's GPT API to provide smart meal planning suggestions based on user input.

  • This AI feature processes only the text input you provide during the session.
  • No input is stored or linked to your identity.
  • AI features are optional and are used solely to improve user experience.

4. Future Changes

If we introduce features that collect analytics or personal data, this policy will be updated accordingly. Users will be informed via the app or app store listings.
